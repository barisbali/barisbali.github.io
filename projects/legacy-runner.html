LEGACY RUNNER

Endless Runner with a Twist

ABOUT THE GAME

🏃 Legacy Runner
Run. Age. Die. Retry.

Legacy Runner isn't just another endless runner, it's a journey through life itself.

Start as a child with boundless energy, grow into your prime as an adult, and finally face your twilight years as an elder. But death isn't the end, it's just the beginning. Every moral choice you make echoes into your next life, and the power-ups you collect? They only activate after you die.

KEY FEATURES

  • Age Progression System - Physically age from child to elder as you run, with each life stage changing your abilities
  • Moral Choice System - Face dilemmas at mysterious portals that determine your eternal fate
  • Death-Activated Power-Ups - Collect orbs in one life, unlock them in the next, build your legacy across reincarnations
  • Fast-Paced Action - Perfect for quick 2-5 minute sessions packed with intensity
  • Smooth Mobile Controls - Intuitive swipe controls designed for on-the-go gaming

HOW TO PLAY

Controls:
  • Swipe Left/Right → Change lanes
  • Swipe Up → Jump over obstacles
  • Swipe Down → Slide under barriers
The Loop:
  • Run forward automatically through procedurally generated obstacles
  • Dodge, jump, and slide to survive
  • Collect coins and mysterious power-up orbs
  • Age every 500 meters (Child → Adult → Elder)
  • Face moral choices at glowing portals
  • Die, reincarnate, and unlock the power-ups you collected
