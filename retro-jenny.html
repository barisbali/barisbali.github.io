RETRO JENNY

What is Retro Jenny?

Retro Jenny is your fun, AI-powered companion designed to assist, entertain, and engage through natural conversation and personality-rich interactions.

Main Features

  • Conversational AI with LLM technology.
  • Cheerful and customizable personality.
  • Automation of simple everyday tasks.
  • Emotional intelligence for empathetic interactions.
  • Easy integration into apps and websites.

Use Cases

Ideal for entertainment, productivity apps, customer service, or as an engaging digital assistant—perfect for fun when you're bored.

This experimental project helps me understand NLP and ML applications. I handle every aspect—from visual design to model training. I always wanted my own AI companion. And yes, I'm a Tony Stark fan!
